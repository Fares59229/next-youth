About Us

Next Youth is a youth-led marketing agency where young adults work for a day at local businesses to promote them through fun, authentic social media videos.

We manage marketing and ads by creating engaging Reels that showcase real moments of us working, gaining experience, and delivering entertaining content to our audience aged 16-30.

This unique approach builds brand awareness while connecting with youth through genuine, relatable storytelling.
